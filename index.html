Biryani

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Spices
  • Yogurt
  • Oil
  • Salt

Preparation Steps

  • Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  • Fry onions until golden brown.
  • Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until done.
  • Cook rice separately until 70% done.
  • Layer the chicken and rice in a pot, cover, and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad.
Cooking Time Servings
1 hour 4 people
